About King of the Hill

Take on other BSC wallets to reign supreme. The King Of The Hill is the one-and-only big spender, the top of the class, no brakes, all gas.

The Perks

As King, you reign supreme over the site. Your name, wallet address, and up to 6 social links can be added for all to see. And for every hour you hold the throne, you earn 1 KING. But watch out! You know everyone's coming for that crown!

How it works

To become King of the Hill, it takes commitment. You must pony up enough BNB to take down the current-reigning champ.

What happens to the old King? Whatever HP was left when they were dethroned will be converted back to BNB and returned to them, minus a flat fee (0.025 BNB).

Don't have the resources? Don't sweat it. Being king ain't no walk in the park. The king's HP decays over time, and once it's within your reach, you can take the throne.
